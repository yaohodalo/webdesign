Our Mission

One Hour. United Worldwide.

Our Lord asked His disciples in the Garden of Gethsemane, "Could you not watch with me one hour?" This platform exists to make that one hour easier — to find a chapel, pledge your time, and join a global family of adorers praying before the Blessed Sacrament every hour of every day.

We are not building a movement. We are joining one — older than us, larger than us, and still answering the question Jesus first asked in the garden.
